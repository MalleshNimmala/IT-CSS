The repeat() Function

Here, repeat(2, 60px 1fr) creates two columns; column1 will take up 60px and column2 takes up one fraction of the available space, and this is repeated for all columns.

This will be the same as writing grid-template-columns: 60px 1fr 60px 1fr;
